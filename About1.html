Who we are?

We are the trust named (Gwalior Hospital Education & Charitable trust)established with the aim to Help Children Of India in the village Gwalior,India.
The trust is Founded by Dr Bal Krishna Sharma retired paediatric Surgeon in the NHS,UK.

Why Help Children Of India?

Help Children Of India is the name of the campaign of Gwalior Hospital Education & Charitable trust to support the disabled, abandoned, homeless residents of Gwalior,India who otherwise would have been forced to live on streets with nobody else to support for food and shelter

Why Snehalaya?

Snehalaya is the name given to our Care home and School, We provide a better care to the disabled orphans,abandoned children,homeless women and educate the dropout children and other needy children in near by villages in Gwalior,India. Gwalior is one of the least developed areas of central India

What we have done so far?

We give them a better life through our school & care home in Gwalior,India which is one of the least developed areas of central India. We started a mainstream school to educate them, to empower them, so that they can lead the life of their own choice and make it better not only for themselves, but also for their whole family and future generations.
